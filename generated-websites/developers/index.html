Start Developing for Edge Today

The MobiledgeX Platform enables developers to dynamically scale and deploy applications on Telco data centers (cloudlets) geographically close to your end-users. Deploying to MobiledgeX's cloudlets provides applications the advantage of low latency, which can be extremely useful for real-time applications such as Augmented Reality, Mobile Gaming, Self-Driving Cars, Drones, etc.

Have an application that could benefit from edge computing? Our team would love to see it! Schedule a time to chat with a member of our developer team and receive early access to MobiledgeX!

Why MobiledgeX?

With our Platform, we provide the power and flexibility to deploy Edge applications across Telco networks, in 3 steps:

  1. Upload your Containers or VM images to our platform
  2. Define your application and deploy instances of it across multiple data centers
  3. Find the closest application instance using our SDKs
